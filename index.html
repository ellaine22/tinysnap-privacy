Privacy Policy

TinySnap Puzzles is a puzzle app designed for young children (ages 5 and under).
Last updated: June 10, 2026

Information We Collect

TinySnap Puzzles does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any user registration.

Advertising

TinySnap Puzzles does not display any advertisements. No third-party advertising SDKs are included in the app.

In-App Purchases

TinySnap Puzzles offers a one-time purchase to unlock all puzzles. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for children ages 5 and under and is listed in the Apple App Store Kids Category. We do not collect any personal information from children. The app functions entirely offline and requires no internet connection for gameplay. A parental gate is required before any in-app purchase can be made.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
